Kegrig

Automated Cocktail Mixer

Project Description

Making your own drink is a whiskey business. That is why my team and I decided to build an automated drink mixer that will do all the work for you. The system is comprised of an android tablet, an Arduino Mega, and 10 motor pumps. A touchscreen interface allows you to select a cocktail drink from our list. You also that the ability to choose your own individual ingredients. Once you select your drink, you can adjust the amount of each ingredient. The tablet sends signals to the Arduino which powers the motors sitting behind the box.

The Kegrig allows for up to 10 ingredients to be used at once. It is also possible to change up the ingredients in case you have a large collection of ingredients you want to use. Check out some of the videos below.
