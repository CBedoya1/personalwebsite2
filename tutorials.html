VLSI Tutorial Series

Supplemental Classroom Instruction

Description

A good instructor can make a difference in a student's education. While taking EECS391 (VLSI Systems Design), I noticed that many of my classmates had dropped the course simply because they struggled working with Cadence Virtuoso, the software used for all the labs and projects. No instructor in the department had experience with the software and the online resources at the time were not very helpful either. When I was the Teaching Assistant for the course the following year, I feared that my students would struggle as well and be discouraged to continue on in the course. Thus, I created a set of YouTube videos dedicated to instructing students in the class on how to use the software. Some of the videos are given below. Retention in the course was at an all-time high and the videos are still reference for classroom instruction.
